About this project

This project basically utilized D3 library in Javascript to visualize the market share of six different toy brands in China from 2008 to 2012, based on subgroups of six different cities. The slides are shown in order of time (years). From each barchart and the slideshow, we can take some analysis on the increasing or decreasing trends of each brands, based on different parameters. In the future updates, the sorted method and dynamic transmission are planned to be implemented.
